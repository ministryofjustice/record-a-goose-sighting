'Record a goose sighting' accessibility exercise instructions

'Record a goose sighting' is a made-up government service, filled with accessibility issues. Your task is to find them all!

To help find the issues, you will need to use the WAVE accessibility plugin with either Chrome or Firefox. You'll also need to try to complete the exercise using just your keyboard. You should also read these tips on what to look for and how to record your findings.

There are 6 pages in total:

  • Start page
  • Do you like geese?
  • What type of goose did you see?
  • When did you see the goose?
  • Check your answers
  • Confirmation page

Accessibility issues start on the next page. Good luck!
